Take one tablet daily, at any time, with or without a meal. What is the science and technology behind Nutrilite™ Vitamin B Dual-Action? Nutrilite™ Vitamin B Dual-Action has been designed to offer an effective, efficient, and more convenient way to get eight essential B vitamins in a spirulina-based formula. The instant release technology in one layer allows the body to access vitamins B2 and B12 quickly and completely, for best absorption. The remaining six B vitamins in the other layer are released slowly and steadily through the gut for efficient transport into the body.

Where do the B vitamins come from?

Many of the B vitamins are sourced from spirulina, a type of blue-green algae. Spirulina is cultured and harvested at a partner facility located in the Sonoran Desert near the Salton Sea in southern California. They are masters of aquatic agriculture, who have passed a rigorous Nutricert™ certification audit. Nutricert™ is our exclusive certification that ensures that sustainable environmental and agricultural practices – consistent with Nutrilite™ values and quality standards – are followed in the cultivation and production of botanicals used to make ingredients intended for Nutrilite™ supplements. These practices set Nutrilite™ Vitamin B Dual-Action apart.

What does bi-layer mean?

The term bi-layer simply refers to the unique, physical structure of the vitamin B tablet. There are two distinct layers: a dark green layer for instant release of two of the B vitamins and a light green layer for the extended release of the remaining six B vitamins.

What does dual-action mean?

The term dual-action refers to the combination of two technologies in one tablet. The dark green layer is the instant release layer that completely releases two B vitamins within a one-hour period immediately after taking. The light green layer is the extended release layer that releases six B vitamins during an eight-hour period.

Is this product all-natural?

No. In addition to using natural spirulina, Nutrilite™ Vitamin B Dual-Action contains synthetic B vitamins to deliver target nutrient levels and ensure consistent potency throughout shelf life. In this way, it leverages the best of nature and the best of science.

Is the product organic?

No, the entire formula is not organic. However, the key botanical used in the product – spirulina – is grown and harvested on a Nutricert™ certified partner farm.
