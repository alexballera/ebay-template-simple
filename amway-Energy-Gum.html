Frequently Asked Questions

How is this product different from Nutrilite® Rhodiola I used to take?

The formula is exactly the same. It made sense to place this product in the XS™ Sports Nutrition category so we simply rebranded the product and gave the product a new name.

Does this product contain caffeine?

Yes. This product contains 75 mg of caffeine per tablet, which is about the amount found in a cup of coffee. A daily dose of two tablets provides 150 mg of caffeine.

Where is the caffeine in XS™ Energy + Focus obtained?

The caffeine in XS™ Energy + Focus is obtained from green tea extract.

What potential beneficial effects does caffeine have in the body?

Caffeine helps maximize both physical endurance and mental focus.

Why was 150 mg/day (provided by two tablets) of caffeine chosen for this product?

The level of caffeine in this product was chosen to promote physical and mental focus during workouts without the side effects that are associated with consuming higher levels of caffeine.

Which, if any, of the ingredients in XS™ Energy + Focus are grown or produced at Nutrilite organic farms?

The Acerola Concentrate is produced from acerola cherries grown on the Nutrilite farm in Brazil. The Spinach Dehydrate is grown and processed at the Nutrilite Rancho El Petacal farm in Jalisco, Mexico. None of the other primary ingredients are grown or produced at Nutrilite farms at this time.

Use Instructions

Take 1 or 2 tablets before physical activity. Do not exceed 2 tablets per day.
